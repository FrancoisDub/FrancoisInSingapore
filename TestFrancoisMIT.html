Discover our services

We Finance Rotables

We finance valuable materials in the scope of your aircraft program development, including and not limited to initial provisioning, spare engines or any new or used materials you need to procure to support your operations

We Structure Transactions

We identify with you materials that can be the underlying asset base for sale and leaseback transactions — or any complex financial asset-based deals. The bottom line is you get fresh funds without impacting your operations

We Offer Material Pool Services

We own or control certain critical, heavy components (engines, nacelles, landing gear shipsets) you would like to get an access to, increasing the reliability of your operations or the protection of your fleet

We Trade Surplus Materials

We help you to liquidate unused assets throughout optimized and tailor-made solutions
